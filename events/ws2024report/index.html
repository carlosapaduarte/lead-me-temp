LEAD-ME Winter Training School Trabzon 2024 Report

Advancing Media Accessibility through Research Methods and Innovative Approaches

8-10 May 2024

Karadeniz Technical University

The COST Action CA19142 LEAD-ME Winter Training School was held from May 8-10, 2024, at Karadeniz Technical University in Trabzon, Turkey. The event, titled "Advancing Media Accessibility through Research Methods and Innovative Approaches," gathered 28 participants to enhance their understanding and research capabilities in media accessibility. The first day focused on basic concepts and qualitative research methods, with sessions led by Prof. Dr. Emre Erdoğan and a practical workshop where participants developed research methods.

The second and third days concentrated on applied research, practical skills, and new technologies in media accessibility. Assoc. Prof. Dr. Ali Şükrü Özbay provided insights into effective academic writing, while Assoc. Prof. Dr. Muhammet Berigel and Serdar Özkaş led a workshop on creating accessible multimedia content. The event concluded with presentations on innovative technologies and eye-tracking, presented by Krzysztof Krejtz, Marta Brescia Zapata, Christopher Hughes, and Assoc. Prof. Dr. Ekrem Bahçekapılı, followed by a certificate ceremony and networking opportunities.

Summary of assessment report:

The Training school on media accessibility highlighted the value of various sessions, particularly the practical workshops on creating accessible multimedia content and eye-tracking technologies, which were deemed highly beneficial by participants. The introductory keynote was praised for its comprehensive overview and meaningful engagement. Suggestions for future events included better preparation for participants' accessible needs, more real-life examples, and enhanced collaborative opportunities. Overall, the event was well-organized and highly appreciated, with specific recommendations aimed at further improving the experience for future attendees.
