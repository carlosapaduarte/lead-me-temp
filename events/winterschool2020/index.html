LEAD-ME Winter Training School 2020

Media Accessibility: Communication for all

23 to 26 November 2020, ONLINE

Delivered by UAB (Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona) in partnership with Cost CA19142 LEAD-ME partners.

Check the Programme of the LEAD-ME Winter School 2020

The LEAD-ME winter training school 2020 Media Accessibility: Communication for all will take place ONLINE (23-27 November 2020).

Applications for the 2020 Media accessibility winter school will open in November 2020.

The online LEAD-ME Winter School for young researchers aims to boost their competences and to ampliate their networks giving them further chances to share ideas creating new tools in providing accessible contents in media.

LEAD-ME winter training school 2020 includes many regular activities in research and training plus a few different sessions unique to the new online format:

  • Plenary panels with university and industry professionals
  • Short talks on hot topics / short lectures (15-20 minutes + 15 minutes Q&A + 5 minute break) about theoretical and practical aspects.
  • Using eye tracking in media accessibility research.
  • Training in Media Accessibility: vocational vs academic and the new forms of accessibility training
  • Funding opportunities and forthcoming events.
