LEAD-ME Winter Training School 2020 Programme

Media Accessibility: Communication for all

23 to 26 November 2020, ONLINE

Delivered by UAB (Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona) in partnership with Cost CA19142 LEAD-ME partners.

  • Day1: Monday 23rd November
  • Day2: Tuesday 24th November
  • Day3: Wednesday 25th November
  • Day4: Thursday 26th November

Monday 23rd November

On the first day of the training school the theoretical and methodological background upon which experimental research is based, the various research options to choose from, the design experimental set up, and then some concrete examples will be presented through ongoing research and training EU funded projects.
