Venue

SWPS University
Ul. Chodakowska 19/31
Warsaw, Poland

More information about the SWPS University campus

Rooms are equipped with full HyFlex and Google Jamboards for collaboration

Online Meet:

The online meeting will follow the same schedule as the in-person meeting. We will use Google Meet to facilitate our meeting. Participants intending to discuss potential collaboration with co-authors will have the opportunity to meet and speak with one another.

Flying to Warsaw

There are two international airports in Warsaw. The transportation from these airports to the city center is provided in the links. You can take a taxi or use public transit.

  • Warsaw Chopin Airport
  • Modlin Airport

Additional information

Polish VISA requirements

If you are a citizen of an EU member state within the Schengen agreement you need to have your national ID or passport to enter Poland. All non-EU country citizens, please check this information on VISA requirements.

Language

We speak Polish, but most people speak English in hotels, shops, restaurants, and tourist places.

Weather

The average monthly temperature in August – the last summer month in Poland – is 18,5°C / 65°F, and the average monthly rainfall level is 66 mm. In Poland, the name of the month (sierpień) comes from the sickle (sierp) used during the harvest, which falls in Poland in August.

Emergency

You can dial 112 in case of an emergency.
