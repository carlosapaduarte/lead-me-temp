Videos

Welcome to the LEAD-ME Action's video series showcasing how people with disabilities interact with digital products. These videos, created by LEAD-ME, provide valuable insights into the real-world experiences and challenges faced by users with disabilities. Our aim is to foster greater understanding and innovation in digital accessibility, helping developers and designers create more inclusive technologies.
